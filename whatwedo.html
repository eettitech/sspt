Safety Locker with Alarm

The Customized Center Locking with Alarm is one of our own invention to prevent the theft and in this way, the additional lock is directly attached with vehicle Chassis with special type of key's. The Alarm system indicates driver about the unauthorize activities. Thus in this way theft/crimes can be reduced to a great extent as vehicles/Materials today are being stolen in large number. Hence, vehicles today require high security which can be achieved with the help of this system.
